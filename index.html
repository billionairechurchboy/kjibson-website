Our services

What we handle for you

Admission, visa processing, accommodation, and flight tickets — everything you need to study in Serbia.

01

University admission

We guide you from first inquiry to enrollment letter. That includes choosing the right Serbian university, preparing your application form, collecting transcripts and recommendation letters, and submitting everything on time.

Our team checks every document before it goes to admissions so rejections from missing or incorrect paperwork are avoided.

02

Visa processing

Once you are admitted, we handle the student visa process — compiling embassy requirements, reviewing your financial and academic documents, and preparing you for your appointment.

We stay in contact with you through approval so you know exactly when you can travel to Serbia.

03

Accommodation

We arrange student housing or dormitory placement close to your campus in Belgrade or other university cities. Options are matched to your budget, program length, and preferred living setup.

You arrive with a confirmed place to stay instead of searching on your own after landing.

04

Flight tickets

We book your return flights to Serbia with dates aligned to your program start and visa approval. That includes coordinating layovers, baggage allowances, and any schedule changes if your intake date shifts.

One less thing to organise before you begin your studies abroad.

Admission checklist

Every step of your application

We manage the full admission pipeline for Serbian universities. Here is the process we guide you through — from your first form to the final decision.

  1. 1.

    Application form submission

    We help you complete and submit your university application with accurate personal and academic details.

  2. 2.

    Transcript upload

    Official transcripts are collected, verified, and uploaded in the format each university requires.

  3. 3.

    Recommendation letter submission

    We coordinate with your referees and ensure recommendation letters reach the admissions office on time.

  4. 4.

    Application fee payment

    Application fees are processed correctly and receipts are filed as proof of payment for your file.

  5. 5.

    Admission decision notification

    Once a decision is issued, we notify you immediately and begin visa processing for your study permit.
